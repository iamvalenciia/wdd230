Description of the Target Audience

A target audience is a group of people who like to start a business and wants to strengthen their relationship with the local businesses

Two Personas

Persona 1: A small entrepreneur without enough money. A person who wants to increase their business and build a strong relationship with the local businesses.

Persona 2: University students, searching for jobs. A person who wants to get experience by working on a local business.

Five Listed Scenarios

Scenario 1: Camilo is a 22-year man from Esmeraldas with a small business raising cows. He could use the website (esmechamber.com) to identify local shops that want to buy milk from their cows.

Scenario 2: Carla is a 24 young woman studying at the local university. She wants to get a job, therefore, she uses the website (esmechamber.com) to know if a local business is hiring.

Scenario 3: Emily loves to work in their business. She has a local shop and wants to build networking with local businesses. She uses the website (esmechamber.com) to find out when there will be a fair to meet local businesses.

Scenario 4: Juan is 40 years old and recently moved to Esmeraldas city, and he is searching for a job in the local community. He uses the website (esmechamber.com) to know if a local business is hiring.

Scenario 5: Gonzalo has a big company. He uses the website (esmechamber.com) to register and participate in a local fair thus improving their relationship with the community.
